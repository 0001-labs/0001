SCT

Draft for approval

Overview

SCT needed a method for calculating CO2e emissions for material line items, connecting invoice data, material weights, transport assumptions, and verified emission factors.

Work

Defined the calculation method, mapped product and material data to emission-factor sources, planned unit handling across weight and transport-related inputs, and documented the expected output for invoices and methodology review.

Outcome

The work created a transparent implementation plan for CO2e reporting, including prioritization between product-specific EPD/LCA data and fallback climate database mappings.
