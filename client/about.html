About

0001 was founded in 2024 with a simple mission: build digital products that feel effortless. We believe great software should disappear into the background, letting people focus on what matters.

We're a small team that values craft over scale. Every pixel, every interaction, every line of code is intentional. We delete more than we keep.

Our products are built for solo builders and small teams who want to operate at scale without the overhead. Local-first, zero-latency, designed for the way people actually work.
