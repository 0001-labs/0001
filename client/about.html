About

0001 was founded in 2026 in Tokyo to build software that scales.

We believe products should be focused and designed as tools, tools that, when done right, eventually become a natural extension of the user's intent.

We design component-first architectures and help teams stay performant so their businesses can scale progressively.

0001 is half in-house products, half consultancy.

We're ready to connect, whether you need a faster, stronger online presence or want to take a deep dive into your current stack to optimize it.
